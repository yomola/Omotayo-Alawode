About Woman Arise and Shine Network

The Woman Arise and Shine Network (WAS Network) is a thriving ministry founded by Pastor Omotayo Alawode, established to inspire, instruct, and empower women to rise into their God-ordained identity and destiny.

Through this dynamic platform, we are raising a generation of spiritually grounded, purpose-driven women leaders who are transforming their homes, communities, and professional spheres with godly wisdom and excellence.

Our vision is to see women:

  • Fully awakened to their divine purpose
  • Equipped with biblical truth and practical wisdom
  • Empowered to impact their spheres of influence
  • Living boldly in their God-given identity

Rooted in Scripture and led by the Holy Spirit, WAS Network provides a sisterhood of support, mentorship, and spiritual growth opportunities.

Our Programs

WAS Prayer Gatherings

Monthly prayer meetings where women intercede for families, nations, and the fulfillment of divine destinies.

Purpose Discovery Workshops

Interactive sessions helping women uncover their God-given gifts and develop them for maximum impact.

Kingdom Wives Fellowship

Support and mentorship for married women building godly homes and Kingdom legacies.

Marketplace Ministry

Equipping professional women to excel in their careers while maintaining spiritual vitality.

Daughter of the King Retreats

Annual spiritual retreats for refreshing, renewal, and deeper encounters with God.

Community Impact

Outreach programs demonstrating God's love through practical service initiatives.
