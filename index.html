Meet Pastor Omotayo Alawode

A passionate speaker, transformational leader, and firebrand woman of God, Omotayo is driven by the Holy Spirit and anchored in Scripture. She founded the Woman Arise Network (WAS Network)—a thriving, multi-dimensional ministry established to inspire, instruct, and empower women to rise into their God-ordained identity and destiny.

With a Master's in Leadership and over a decade in financial services, she uniquely bridges ministry and marketplace wisdom. She and her husband, Pastor Emmanuel, co-pastor RCCG His Presence in Newcastle and are parents to two beautiful children.

  • Founder, Woman Arise Network (empowering women leaders)
  • Marriage Counselor & Mentor (for singles and couples)
  • Chair of Trustees for a UK charity
  • Speaker & Strategist blending spiritual and professional growth
